Autumn is the best season

Autumn, also known as fall in North American English, is one of the four temperate seasons on Earth. Outside the tropics, autumn marks the transition from summer to winter, in September (Northern Hemisphere) or March (Southern Hemisphere).

Autumn is the season when the duration of daylight becomes noticeably shorter and the temperature cools considerably.
